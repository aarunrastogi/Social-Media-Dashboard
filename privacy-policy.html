Privacy Policy

At Social Media Dashboard, we value your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Data We Collect

We collect data from social media platforms (Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn, etc.) through their public APIs.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is displayed on our dashboard for viewing purposes only. We do not store or share any personal information.

How We Protect Your Data

We do not store any personal data. All data is retrieved in real-time from social media platforms and is not kept on our servers.
